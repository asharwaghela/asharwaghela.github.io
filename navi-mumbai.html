Our Textile Reuse Mission

♻️ Responsible Textile Reuse

Our primary goal is to help reusable garments find a second life instead of ending up in landfills. By extending garment life cycles, we support a more sustainable and responsible approach to clothing management.

🌱 Sustainability Focus

Textile reuse helps reduce environmental waste, lowers pressure on landfills and encourages responsible consumption practices. Every reusable garment collected contributes to a more circular economy.

💰 Collection & Valuation

Eligible wearable garments may be evaluated between ₹15–₹20 per KG. Final valuation depends on garment condition, quantity and overall reusability.
